Our Story

A local Osoyoos stop with real Mexican soul.

Sofia's Mexican Food is the kind of place you discover once and keep coming back to. Tucked inside the Osoyoos Fruit Basket on Crowsnest Highway, it's a small local spot serving authentic Mexican street food with fresh ingredients and bold flavors.

Whether you're a local or a road tripper passing through the beautiful Okanagan, Sofia's delivers a taste of genuine Mexican comfort food — warm, satisfying, and made with love.
