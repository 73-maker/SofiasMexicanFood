🌵 Our Story

A Hidden Gem in the Heart of Osoyoos

Sofia's Mexican Food is the kind of place you discover once and keep coming back to. Tucked inside the Osoyoos Fruit Basket on Crowsnest Highway, it's a beloved local spot serving authentic Mexican street food with fresh ingredients and bold, unforgettable flavors.

Whether you're a local Osoyoos resident or a road tripper exploring the beautiful Okanagan Valley, Sofia's delivers a taste of genuine Mexican comfort food — warm, generous, and made with love. Rated 4.7 stars by over 574 guests on Google.
